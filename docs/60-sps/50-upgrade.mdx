# Upgrade DPS to SPS

* Click the main object for your avatar in the hierarchy on the left
* At the top of the window, click `Tools > VRCFury > SPS > Upgrade DPS to SPS`
* A window should notify you of which objects will be upgraded. Be sure all your haptic zones are listed, then click OK!
When your avatar is selected, you should see orange gizmos in the 3D View enveloping your the upgraded haptic zones.
* Upload your avatar and you're done!

> Note: If your avatar was previously setup using Fluff's Toolbox DPS Setup or Universal DPS Attachment System, your menu toggles may move into a new folder named `SPS`.
